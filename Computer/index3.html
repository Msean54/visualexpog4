Data Methodology




Business Understanding:
Question 1: Which gender has the highest number of patients diagnosed with lung cancer, according to the survey?

Question 2: What is the youngest age of the person that deals with lung cancer?



Analytic Approach:
This information will be used to reply to the business comprehension questions that we have developed. This information will also be utilized in order for visitors to our website to get an idea or learn more about how these folks were diagnosed with lung cancer.



Data Requirement:
1. Gender: M(male), F(female)
2. Age: Age of the patient
3. Smoking: YES=2 , NO=1.
4. Yellow fingers: YES=2 , NO=1.
5. Anxiety: YES=2 , NO=1.
6. Peer_pressure: YES=2 , NO=1.
7. Chronic Disease: YES=2 , NO=1.
8. Fatigue: YES=2 , NO=1.
9. Allergy: YES=2 , NO=1.
10. Wheezing: YES=2 , NO=1.
11. Alcohol: YES=2 , NO=1.
12. Coughing: YES=2 , NO=1.
13. Shortness of Breath: YES=2 , NO=1.
14. Swallowing Difficulty: YES=2 , NO=1.
15. Chest pain: YES=2 , NO=1.
16. Lung Cancer: YES , NO.




Data Collection:
The data set utilized comes from Mysar Ahmad Bhat of Kaggle (a platform where we can get many data sets), and it is tagged cancer, pharmaceuticals and treatments, hospitals, and treatment centers. We obtained the data set from Mysar's survey, which was updated three months ago.



Data Understanding:
The data gathered that is shown includes a medical history of each individual. This data collected from a survey type record includes the details concerning any medical issues and symptoms that may/have not come about to lung disease. This data is enough to represent the needed sample basis for questions above.



Data Preparation:
The additional effort required to alter and interact with the data is to perform some data cleansing to ensure that the information included within the data set is current and to eliminate any duplicates that may exist. Following that, the data set will be changed into a more ordered format to ensure that the vital data can be found quickly.
